Roblox

Roblox is a massive online game creation platform and social ecosystem, launched in 2006, where users play and create millions of user-generated games. Primarily popular with children, it functions as a social network, game engine (Roblox Studio), and virtual economy using "Robux" currency. It is free-to-play across mobile, consoles, and PC.
